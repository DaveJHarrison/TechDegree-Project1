About Me

I am married with 2 grown up children who are both pursuing their own careers in the digital world.

My career to date has been spent working in electrical engineering, telecom engineering and latterly product development/management. I have now reached the point where I would like to work for myself doing something that I think I will enjoy immensely. Hence my decision to re-train as a front-end developer.

I am a highly motivated and results-orientated individual who has the ability to lead matrix teams to deliver complex projects. I possess a strong business and commercial focus gained in various product and project management roles. Excellent track record in developing and launching projects/products in tight timescales. When I have finished this course, I hope I will be able to say that I am well on the way to being a competent front-end developer.
